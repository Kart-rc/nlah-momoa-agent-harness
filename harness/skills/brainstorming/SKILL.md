# Skill — Brainstorming

## Purpose

Generate multiple candidate approaches before plan lock-in.

## Workflow

1. Restate goal and non-goals.
2. Produce at least three viable approaches.
3. Evaluate tradeoffs: complexity, risk, validation burden.
4. Record selected option and rejected options with reasons.

## Required artifacts

- `DESIGN_OPTIONS.md`
- decision note in `DECISION_RECORD.md`

## Exit criteria

A selected approach exists with explicit tradeoff rationale and at least one fallback option.
