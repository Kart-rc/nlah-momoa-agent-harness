# Skill — Evidence-Backed Answering

## Purpose

Ensure final claims are grounded in artifacts, diffs, tests, and validations.

## Workflow

1. Enumerate material claims.
2. Attach each claim to direct evidence path.
3. Mark unsupported claims as unknown or pending.
4. Remove or qualify speculative statements.

## Required checks

- Every completion claim has at least one artifact or test reference.
- Known limitations are surfaced in `RELEASE_PACKET.md` and `RESPONSE.md`.
