# Stage 01 — Ambiguity Resolution

## Owner

Ambiguity Facilitator

## Purpose

Resolve ambiguity that materially changes scope, architecture, cost, risk, implementation path, validation tier, or audience fit.

## Inputs

- `TASK.md`
- `TASK_CLASSIFICATION.md`
- known constraints

## Outputs

- `AMBIGUITY_REGISTER.md`
- `DESIGN_OPTIONS.md`, when multiple materially different paths exist
- `DECISION_RECORD.md`, when a decision is made

## Allowed Actions

- Ask targeted questions.
- Record assumptions when immediate progress is more valuable than blocking.
- Escalate unresolved ambiguity to scope contract as an explicit risk.

## Gate

Planning is blocked only when unresolved ambiguity could cause materially wrong work.

## Next Stage Rule

Proceed to scope contract after critical ambiguity is resolved or documented with an explicit assumption.
