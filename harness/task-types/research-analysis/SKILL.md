# Research / Analysis Harness

## Purpose

Execute evidence-first research tasks with explicit hypotheses, source quality checks, and uncertainty reporting.

## Required Inputs

- research question or analysis objective
- scope boundaries and decision context
- preferred sources or constraints, if any

## Required Artifacts

- `RESEARCH_QUESTION.md`
- `SOURCE_LOG.md`
- `HYPOTHESES.md`
- `ANALYSIS_NOTES.md`
- `FINDINGS_SUMMARY.md`
- `LIMITATIONS.md`
- `RELEASE_PACKET.md`

## Workflow

1. Clarify question and non-goals
2. Build source plan and quality criteria
3. Gather evidence
4. Test competing hypotheses
5. Synthesize findings with confidence levels
6. Validate claims and cite sources
7. Publish limitations and open questions

## Validation Gates

- major claims are source-backed
- contradictory evidence is acknowledged
- confidence and uncertainty are explicit
- final recommendations map to evidence
