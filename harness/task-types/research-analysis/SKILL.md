# Research / Analysis Harness

## Purpose

Execute evidence-first research tasks with explicit hypotheses, source quality checks, and uncertainty reporting.

## Required Inputs

- research question or analysis objective
- scope boundaries and decision context
- preferred sources or constraints, if any

## Required Artifacts

- `RESEARCH_QUESTION.md`
- `SOURCE_LOG.md`
- `HYPOTHESES.md`
- `ANALYSIS_NOTES.md`
- `FINDINGS_SUMMARY.md`
- `LIMITATIONS.md`
- `RELEASE_PACKET.md`

## Workflow

1. Clarify question and non-goals
2. Build source plan and quality criteria
3. Gather evidence
4. Test competing hypotheses
5. Synthesize findings with confidence levels
6. Validate claims and cite sources
7. Publish limitations and open questions

## Validation Gates

- major claims are source-backed
- contradictory evidence is acknowledged
- confidence and uncertainty are explicit
- final recommendations map to evidence

## Role Model

- orchestrator: scope research objective and depth tier
- planner: define research method and synthesis milestones
- implementer: gather evidence and build analysis artifacts
- reviewer: stress-test claims and interpretation quality
- independent validator: check citations, methods, and conclusion integrity
- release certifier: approve final analysis and uncertainty notes

## End-to-End Flow Coverage

This task family fully covers harness stages:

1. intake and research-question framing
2. ambiguity resolution for scope and confidence thresholds
3. plan and evidence collection work phases
4. synthesis, review, and dissent resolution
5. independent validation and targeted repairs
6. release certification with limitations and confidence levels

## Release Expectations

Release must include method transparency, source-backed findings, uncertainty disclosure, and recommended next actions.
