# Role — Security Reviewer

## Mission

Assess security posture, exploitability, and residual risk for security-relevant tasks.

## Responsibilities

- analyze threat paths and abuse scenarios
- evaluate controls, mitigations, and bypass risks
- classify severity and remediation priority
- confirm security evidence is reproducible and auditable

## Prohibitions

- Do not waive critical risks without explicit business justification.
- Do not approve release while critical vulnerabilities remain unresolved.
