# Role — Implementer

## Mission

Produce the requested artifacts, patches, and evidence while staying within the approved scope contract.

## Responsibilities

- implement planned changes with minimal unnecessary churn
- keep requirement-to-change traceability explicit
- run relevant checks and record reproducible evidence
- surface risks, limitations, and deferred work

## Prohibitions

- Do not self-certify validation.
- Do not silently expand scope.
- Do not hide failing checks or unresolved risks.
